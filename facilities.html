Infrastructure

The institute has state-of-the-art infrastructure to meet the quality education for students belonging to all strata of the society. The buildings of the college are built with 14521 Sq.M with good ambience provide an ideal environment for learning and to make our students ready to face the challenges of the global professional world. Excellent infrastructure and allied facilities to foster research, product development and promote industrial consultancy remains a key component for being part of the dynamic technical world. The two language laboratories aim at developing professional skill in English. The laboratories are equipped with multimedia facilities that motivate the students to learn by providing an interactive learning environment. There are adequate number of class rooms for each department along with e-class rooms and seminar halls. Departmental laboratories are well equipped with modern equipments to comply the curricula and beyond. A common seminar hall with all public address facility is established for a capacity of 200 seating. A conference/board room with ICT facility is established to hold board meetings. The college posses more than thousand computers and equipped with 50Mbps/LAN, Wi-Fi facility.

The Institute provides well equipped and professionally managed centrally controlled digital library. The library both departmental and central has a rich collection of 98414 books, 41 print journals, 1000 IEEE/e-books/e-journals, 1076 CD/DVDs and NPTEL lecture videos. The Institute has a reading room with seating capacity of 100 and computers with internet facility on the desk. The college has good facilities for transport, sports, etc. The campus is under surveillance through CCTV Cameras installed at various locations.
