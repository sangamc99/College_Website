:: Vision & Mission of Training and Placement Cell ::

Vission:

Harness the young talent based on their knowledge, skills & abilities to make them emerge as successful technologists or technopreneurs of the 21st century, thereby leading the country with their innovative ideas, strength, enduring values, ethical leadership and harmony.

MISSION

1.Enhancing employability of the students through comprehensive induction & training programmes.
2.Exploring & identifying new avenues of improvements by assisting the students to search for prospective profiles in terms of employment.
3.To make Netaji Subhash Engineering College as a preferred destination for the industry to identify & select fresh talent.
4.Create a tech innovation hub to represent a genuinely new and exciting model for providing technical support to the entrepreneurs.
5.Counseling & motivating the new generation to become the flag bearers in joining the teaching community & promote higher education.
